Welcome to Whuffy

At Whuffy we strive to bring the highest quality bucket hats at the lowest possible price. We started in 2007 out of a van and now have 52 stores across New Zealand and Australia. Our company is 100% New Zealand owned and operated. This year we are looking to expand our business with a clothing line. We started this website to give updates and allow delivery during this global pandemic. By the end of year we aim to have released our clothing line and start worldwide shipping to accomodate for any future events. Thank you for sticking with us during this time.

Sincerly, The Whuffy Team.
